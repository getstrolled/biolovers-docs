Biolovers is a **link-in-bio** site. You make one personal page (like `biolovers.site/yourname`) and put everything on it: your name, picture, social accounts, music that plays in the background, your Discord status, gear you use, your portfolio, and more.

You build the page in the **dashboard** at <a href="https://dashboard.biolovers.site" target="_blank" rel="noopener">dashboard.biolovers.site</a>. You don't need to know how to code.

## What your page can have

- **Avatar, name, headline, bio** — the basics.
- **Social link chips** — Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, Twitch, GitHub, Spotify, Discord, and dozens more.
- **Live tiles** — your latest YouTube video, what you're listening to on Spotify, your Steam profile preview, your Twitch live status, etc.
- **Background music** — a song that plays on your page (with mute / play controls visitors can use).
- **Page background** — image, video, or a color.
- **Click-to-enter splash** — the optional "tap to continue" intro screen.
- **Widgets** — markdown notes, embeds, a gear shelf, a portfolio with clips, your Steam inventory.
- **Custom cursor** — replace the mouse arrow with your own image.
- **Earned & custom badges** — small icons next to your name.

The free tier covers all the essentials. **Premium** unlocks extras like custom fonts, multiple audio tracks, custom favicon, alternate domains, and more — see [what Premium unlocks](/premium/what-premium-unlocks).

## Two surfaces, one site

| Where | What it's for | URL |
|-------|---------------|-----|
| **Your public page** | What visitors see when they tap your link. | `biolovers.site/yourname` |
| **The dashboard** | Where you log in and edit your page. | `dashboard.biolovers.site` |

The marketing pages (`biolovers.site` itself — pricing, FAQ, leaderboard) are a third surface, but you don't need to log in there.
