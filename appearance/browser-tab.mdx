## What this panel changes

Two surfaces:

1. **Browser tab** — the title and icon your visitor sees pinned to the top of the browser.
2. **Link previews + Google snippet** — the big card that appears when your URL is pasted into Discord / iMessage / Slack / Twitter, plus the Google search result.

## Custom tab title

Free text up to 70 characters. Empty = default `Biolovers | "@yourname"`.

Examples:

- `My studio`
- `Atlas — sound design`
- `Now playing 🎵`

## Tab title typewriter

Animates the tab title letter-by-letter and (optionally) rotates through extra phrases.

| Control | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| **Phrases (one per line)** | Lines to rotate through. |
| **Speed** | How fast each character types. |
| **Loop** | Replay forever or stop after the last phrase. |

The typewriter on the tab title is part of the same **Premium typewriter** feature used on display name / @handle / bio — it stays disabled on the free tier.

## Favicon

The little icon in the browser tab.

- Formats: **PNG / WebP / GIF / JPEG / SVG**.
- Max **512 KB**.
- Square images crop best (browser tabs are square).

Hit **Remove** to revert to the default Biolovers icon.

## Link preview & SEO

This block controls how your page looks **outside** of biolovers.site — Google search results, Discord/Slack/iMessage embeds, Twitter cards.

### Page description

Up to **320 characters**. Used as:

- The Google snippet shown under your title in search results.
- The subtitle in Discord / iMessage / Twitter previews.

If empty, we use the first ~160 characters of your bio.

### Theme color

A hex color used for:

- The mobile browser address-bar tint when your page is open.
- The colored stripe down the side of Discord / Slack / iMessage previews.

### Share-preview banner (Open Graph image)

The big image at the top of social embeds.

- **Best size:** 1200 × 630 px.
- **Formats:** PNG / JPEG / WebP / GIF.
- **Max:** 2 MB.
- You can upload a file **or** paste an `https://` URL.

If empty, the embed shows **the site's default OG card** — not your avatar. Set this if you want a custom share image.

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My new favicon shows the old one">
    Browsers cache favicons aggressively. Hit Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+R to hard-refresh. On mobile, fully closing the tab and reopening usually clears it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Discord still shows the old preview after I changed it">
    Discord caches embeds for ~24h. To force a refresh, paste the link into a private channel — Discord re-fetches when the URL appears in a place it hasn't seen recently.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is the panel grey?">
    You're on the free tier. See [What Premium unlocks](/premium/what-premium-unlocks) — the Browser tab panel is Premium-only.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I make the tab title rotate forever?">
    Yes — turn on the **Loop** toggle on the typewriter. Note that this can be a battery drain on phones.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
