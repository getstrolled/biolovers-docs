This is the manual for **[biolovers.site](https://biolovers.site)** — your link-in-bio profile, the dashboard where you build it, and the upgrades you can buy on top.

Every page on this site shows you **exactly which button to click**, with a screenshot taken from the real dashboard. No tech-speak.

## Quickstart in 5 minutes

The fastest path from "made an account" to "shareable link". Five clicks, in order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick your username">
    Sign up at <a href="https://dashboard.biolovers.site/register" target="_blank" rel="noopener">dashboard.biolovers.site/register</a>. Your username becomes your link, e.g. `biolovers.site/yourname`. See [Sign up](/getting-started/sign-up).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload an avatar">
    Open the [Avatar panel](/media/avatar). Pick a square picture (PNG / JPEG / GIF / WebP, up to **2 MiB**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a card layout">
    Open [Card layout](/appearance/card-layout). Try the **Default**, **Modern**, **Simplistic**, and **Sleek** presets — pick what looks best with your background.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your social links">
    Open [Social link chips](/connections/social-links). Drop in Instagram / X / TikTok / YouTube — the icons appear instantly under your bio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share your link">
    Visit `https://biolovers.site/yourname` in a new tab. Copy that URL into your Instagram bio / TikTok bio / Twitter pinned tweet. Done.
  </Step>
</Steps>
