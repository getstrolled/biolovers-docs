A scroll page is an **extra widget area below your main card**. Visitors swipe / scroll down to see it. Useful for splitting bio + portfolio + FAQ across distinct screens.

## When to use them

- Your main card is already busy and you want a "page 2".
- You want to dedicate a screen to your portfolio without crowding the bio.
- You're on mobile and want short, scannable sections instead of one long page.

## When NOT to use them

- If you only have 2–3 widgets, just put them on the main card.
- Visitors *will* miss content past the second screen — only put extra-credit content down there.

## Configuring

Scroll pages are part of the [Widgets editor](/widgets/overview). Each page has:

- A **section heading**.
- A list of widgets (drag widgets between sections).

You can have a few sections. The order in the editor matches the scroll order on the public page.

## Tips

- Put a **small "scroll for more" hint** (an arrow icon, the next-section heading) just above the fold so visitors know there's more.
- If your main card has a lot of links, dedicate **page 2** to long-form content (markdown + portfolio).
- Test on mobile — scroll pages feel different on phones than on a 1440 monitor.
