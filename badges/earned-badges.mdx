Site badges are small icons next to your display name on your public profile. Some are **earned** (we add them automatically when you qualify); some are **bought** (Verified, Custom badges); some are **manual** (Owner, Staff).

## Site badges

These are the slugs that can appear next to your name (`Profile.siteBadges`). Labels and icons come from the admin-managed catalog (`BadgeDefinition`).

| Slug | Default label | How you get it |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| `owner` | Owner | Granted by the team — owners of biolovers.site. |
| `staff` | Staff | Granted by the team to staff members. Cannot be bought. |
| `premium` | Premium | Auto-applied while your account has Premium. |
| `donator_bronze` | (catalog) | Lifetime donations ≥ **$10**. |
| `donator_silver` | (catalog) | Lifetime donations ≥ **$50**. |
| `donator_gold` | (catalog) | Lifetime donations ≥ **$100**. |
| `discord_booster` | (catalog) | Auto-synced when you **boost the Biolovers Discord server** (the bot listens for `guildMemberUpdate` / `Add` / `Remove` and toggles the badge). |
| Custom | (your label) | Bought slots, your own image. See [Custom badges](/badges/custom-badges). |

Only the **highest donator tier** ever shows (`applyDonatorTierReplace`) — bronze is replaced by silver when you cross into silver, and so on.

## Discord profile badges (separate)

Quest, Pomelo migration, Nitro flags, HypeSquad, etc. are **Discord** badges fetched from your linked Discord account. They render on the **Discord presence card** (when you've enabled "Show profile badges") — they are **not** site-level badges next to your display name.

## Bought separately

| Badge | How you get it |
|-------|----------------|
| **Verified** | One-time **$19.99** purchase. See [Verified](/premium/verified). Manually granted by staff on application instead. |
| **Custom badges** | Buy slots, upload your own images. See [Custom badges](/badges/custom-badges). |

## Where badges show

- **Next to your display name** on the public profile.
- **Inside the stats line** *(optional)* — toggle on the [Stats panel](/profile/stats-corner) to compact them onto the meta row.
- **Custom badges** appear after earned ones, in the order you set them in the panel.

## Order

Earned badges are sorted by the catalog's `sortOrder` first, then a default `SITE_BADGE_ORDER` (`owner` → `staff` → `donator` → `premium`). Custom badges follow.

## Hover text

Hover any badge to see its `description`. For custom badges this is the **Hover message** field you set when you uploaded the image (max 20 chars).

## Hide a badge

Toggle **Show on profile** off for any badge — it stays earned but disappears from the public page (`siteBadgesHidden` on the profile).

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I have Premium / Verified / Donator but the badge isn't showing">
    Check the **Show on profile** toggle for that badge in this panel. If it's on and still missing, sign out + sign back in to refresh your session.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does the donator badge upgrade tiers?">
    The instant a payment lands. So if you donate $5 today and $5 tomorrow, the bronze badge appears the moment your lifetime crosses $10.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the booster badge based on Nitro?">
    No — it's based on **boosting the Biolovers Discord server**. Linking Discord without boosting doesn't grant it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I hide a badge I earned?">
    Yes — toggle **Show on profile** off for that badge. The badge stays earned, just doesn't appear publicly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are the Quest / Pomelo badges?">
    Those are **Discord** profile badges, shown on the Discord presence card (when "Show profile badges" is on). They aren't site badges next to your display name.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
