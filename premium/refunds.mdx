## General rule

Fees are **final and non-refundable** except where:

- law in your jurisdiction requires a remedy;
- we made a clear billing error (duplicate charge for the same product, charge for a product we never delivered, charge at the wrong amount caused by us);
- we cancel or fail to provide the product within a reasonable time **and** you have not yet used the benefit; or
- we agree to a refund in writing as a courtesy.

There is **no 14-day cooling-off** window written into the policy. Each request is reviewed on its merits against the rule above.

## How to ask for a refund

Open a request through our <a href="https://biolovers.site/contact" target="_blank" rel="noopener">contact form</a> (replies go to your verified email) with:

1. Your username.
2. The order ID or Stripe receipt # / NOWPayments order ID.
3. The reason.

You can also email <a href="mailto:support@biolovers.site">support@biolovers.site</a>.

## Card refunds (Stripe)

Approved card refunds go back to the **same card** through Stripe. They typically appear within a few business days, depending on your bank.

## Crypto refunds (NOWPayments)

Cryptocurrency transactions are **typically irreversible** once confirmed on the network — we cannot reverse an on-chain transfer.

If we agree to refund a crypto purchase, we refund the **equivalent USD value** of the original payment in a coin we support, **at the conversion rate at the time we issue the refund**. **Network fees are not reimbursed.**

If your sending wallet was an exchange deposit address, that exchange may not credit the refund — provide a personal-wallet address instead.

## Disputes (chargebacks)

If you open a chargeback or dispute on a charge we believe is valid (for example you used Premium features, then disputed the charge), we may **suspend the related account, downgrade premium benefits, or restrict purchases** until the dispute is resolved. We may submit evidence to your card network. If the dispute is decided in our favour, the original charge stands.

We strongly prefer you **message us first** — most issues are quicker to resolve directly than via Stripe disputes.

## Account suspension or termination

Fees paid for a product on an account that we later suspend or terminate for a Terms violation are **not refundable**. This includes fraud, abuse, hate or harassment content, attempts to defraud payment networks, ban evasion, and serious or repeated breaches of community rules.
