## Pricing

The **unit price** is `CUSTOM_BADGE_UNIT_USD = $4.99` per slot. There's a **volume discount** that grows with quantity:

| Quantity | Discount | Total | Per slot |
|----------|----------|-------|----------|
| 1 | 0% | $4.99 | $4.99 |
| 2 | 5% | $9.48 | $4.74 |
| 3 | 10% | $13.47 | $4.49 |
| 4 | 15% | $16.97 | $4.24 |
| 5 | 20% | $19.96 | $3.99 |
| ≥ 5 | 20% (capped) | `5 × $4.99 × 0.80 …` | $3.99 |

The discount is `+5% per extra slot after the first, capped at 20%` (`customBadgeVolumeDiscountPercent`).

## Bundles

Two ways to buy:

| Bundle | What you pay | What you get |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| **Standard** | Quantity × unit, with the volume discount | The same number of slots you bought. |
| **5-for-6** | The discounted price for 5 ($19.96) | **6 slots.** A free extra slot on top of the 20% volume discount. |

The **5-for-6** bundle is the best per-slot price.

## How to buy

1. Pick the **bundle** (and quantity if standard).
2. Tap **Purchase**.
3. Pick **Card** or **Crypto** in the modal.
4. Accept Terms + Refund policy.
5. Pay.

Slots appear on your account the moment payment confirms — go to the [Badges panel](/badges/custom-badges) to upload designs.

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is the 5-for-6 bundle the best price?">
    Yes — you pay for 5 (with the 20% volume discount baked in) and get 6 slots, putting the per-slot cost at ~$3.33.

  <Accordion title="Can I buy 2 bundles at once?">
    Buy them one after the other — slots stack on your account. There's no limit on total slots.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I delete a custom badge later?">
    Slots are returned to your pool. You can upload a new design without buying again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I refund unused slots?">
    Yes — unused slots are refundable, *case-by-case*, while still unused. Once a slot has been spent on a badge (or one is being created), that slot is considered delivered. See [Refunds](/premium/refunds).
  </Accordion>
