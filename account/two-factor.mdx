## Two states

| State | What you see |
|-------|--------------|
| **Off** | A **Set up authenticator** button. Tap it for a QR code + secret. |
| **On** | "Two-step is on." plus a **Password (to disable 2FA)** field and a **Disable 2FA** button. |

## Setting it up

The full step-by-step is in [Two-step sign-in](/getting-started/two-factor#turn-it-on-2-minutes). Confirmation button is **Confirm & enable**.

## Turning it off

Type your account password into **Password (to disable 2FA)** and tap **Disable 2FA**.
